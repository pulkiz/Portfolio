About Me

My name is Pulkit Grover, and I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at York University. Originally from Pune, India, I moved to Toronto in 2023 to grow both personally and academically. The transition has been both exciting and challenging, deepening my independence and expanding my worldview. My journey into technology began around age ten when my grandfather introduced me to electronics. Fascinated by how things worked, I began exploring operating systems, rooting and jailbreaking mobile devices, and gradually taught myself programming which was an interest that evolved into a deep passion for computer hardware, systems development, and emerging fields like artificial intelligence. Outside academics, I maintain a balanced lifestyle through fitness, nutrition, and creative outlets like cooking and baking. Soccer keeps me active and grounded in teamwork. Choosing Lassonde School of Engineering was intentional. It aligns with my hands-on, innovation-driven mindset. As I continue developing in this field, I aim to contribute to impactful technologies and meaningful systems. My artifact is my smile which is a symbol of self-belief, resilience, and purpose. It represents the confidence I’ve gained through challenges and serves as a personal reminder to always move forward with optimism.
